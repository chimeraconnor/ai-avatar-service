This document outlines a comprehensive content strategy to launch and rank an AI avatar service for influencers. The strategy focuses on SEO-driven content that targets high-intent keywords, builds authority through case studies, and differentiates from competitors by emphasizing creator monetization.

**Primary Goals:**
1. Rank for core keywords within 6 months
2. Generate 100+ organic leads/month by month 3
3. Establish thought leadership in AI creator tools space
4. Convert 10% of organic traffic to demo signups

## On-Page SEO Best Practices

### Title Tags

- **Length:** 50-60 characters optimal
- **Format:** [Primary Keyword] | [Brand] or [Primary Keyword] | [Secondary Keyword]
- **Examples:**
  - "AI Avatar for Influencers | Monetize Your Following"
  - "How to Create AI Avatar | Complete 2026 Guide"
  - "CarynAI Case Study | $71k Revenue in First Week"

### Meta Descriptions

- **Length:** 150-160 characters optimal
- **Format:** [Value proposition] + [Call to action]
- **Examples:**
  - "Create your AI avatar and monetize your following. Fans pay to chat with your AI clone. Start earning passive income today."
  - "Learn how to create an AI avatar in 30 minutes. Step-by-step guide for OnlyFans, Twitch, and YouTube creators."
  - "CarynAI made $71,610 in first week. Learn from this case study and replicate their success with AI avatars."

### Header Tags

- **H1:** One per page, includes primary keyword
- **H2:** Section headers, include secondary keywords
- **H3:** Subsection headers, related keywords
- **H4-H6:** Less critical, use for structure

### Internal Linking

**From Homepage:**
- "See All Features" → /features
- "See How It Works" → /how-it-works
- "View Pricing" → /pricing
- "Read Case Studies" → /case-studies
- "Read Our Blog" → /blog

**From Blog Posts:**
- Link to related blog posts
- Link to relevant features pages
- Link to case studies
- Link to pricing

**From Case Studies:**
- Link to "Get Started" (pricing)
- Link to "How It Works"
- Link to related blog posts

### Schema Markup

**Implement on all pages:**
- **Organization:** Company info, logo, social links
- **Service:** Service name, description, pricing
- **FAQPage:** Questions and answers (FAQ page)
- **Article:** Blog posts (headline, author, date)
- **BreadcrumbList:** Navigation hierarchy

**Example Schema (Service):**
```json
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "Service",
  "name": "AI Avatar Service for Influencers",
  "description": "Create your AI avatar and monetize your following with chat, voice, and video AI companions.",
  "provider": {
    "@type": "Organization",
    "name": "[Our Brand Name]"
  },
  "offers": {
    "@type": "Offer",
    "price": "499",
    "priceCurrency": "USD",
    "description": "One-time setup fee"
  }
}
```

### Image SEO

- **Alt Text:** Descriptive, includes keywords
- **File Names:** Descriptive (e.g., "ai-avatar-chat-interface.png")
- **Size:** Optimized (Compress images under 500KB)
- **Format:** WebP or PNG (quality 80-90%)

### Page Speed

- **Target:** < 3 seconds load time
- **Tools:** Google PageSpeed Insights, GTmetrix
- **Optimization:**
  - Minify CSS/JS
  - Lazy load images
  - Use CDN
  - Enable compression
