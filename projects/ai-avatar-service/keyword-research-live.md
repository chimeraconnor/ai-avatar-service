# Keyword Research - Live AI Avatar Service (VTuber Model)

## Service Positioning

**What we offer:**
- VTuber model (virtual anime/3D avatar, NOT face cloning)
- Voice cloning (creator's real voice)
- LLM personality cloning (speech patterns, tone, vocabulary)
- Influencer monetization (fans pay to chat)
- Fan experience (speak to AI version of favorite creator)

**What we DON'T do:**
- No face cloning (unlike deepfake services)
- No non-consensual AI creation
- No unauthorized personality cloning
